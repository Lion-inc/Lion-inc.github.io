PRODUCT IDEA

The idea and purpose:

An Android application filled with functionality that will be useful for students of the Faculty of Electronics and Computer Technology and can be integrated into their information system.

Functionality and privilages:

  • Quick access to critical information;
  • One of the methods of notifying students of the faculty;
  • The first and only Android program of the university;
